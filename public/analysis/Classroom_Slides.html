⚠️ Common Student Mistakes

MistakeHow to Fix
Forgetting $ in spreadsheetPractice copying formulas across cells
Wrong logic gate outputAlways draw truth table first
Mixing Primary/Foreign keysPK = identifies records; FK = links tables
HTML closing tag missedAlways check </tag> for every <tag>
Pseudocode infinite loopEnsure counter increments inside loop
Deployment method confusionDirect = stop & switch | Parallel = run both

🏆 Final Success Tips

  • ✅ Study BOTH Grade 10 & Grade 11 textbooks fully
  • ✅ Practice past papers from 2015 onwards
  • ✅ Master the TOP 10 must-know topics first
  • ✅ Always show your working - especially in conversions
  • ✅ Draw clear diagrams with labels
  • ✅ Time yourself when practicing
  • ✅ Don't leave MCQs blank - educated guesses help
